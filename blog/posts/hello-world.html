Hello, World – Why I Started This Blog

🎙️

This is the first post on this blog. I've been meaning to start writing for a while, and I figured the best time to start is now.

What This Blog Is About

I plan to write about things I'm thinking through in my research — ideas that aren't ready to be papers yet, experiments that didn't work but taught me something, and occasionally opinions on the field.

Topics will likely include: machine unlearning, voice privacy, speech synthesis, low-resource NLP, and whatever else I find interesting enough to write about.

Why Write at All?

"Writing is thinking. To write well is to think clearly. That's why it's so hard." — David McCullough

I write because it forces me to be precise. It's easy to have a fuzzy intuition about something; it's much harder to explain it clearly to someone else. The act of writing reveals the gaps.

I also write for my future self — these posts are notes I'd want to have when I come back to a problem six months later.

If something here is useful to you, or if you disagree with something I've written, feel free to reach out. You can find my contact info on the homepage.
